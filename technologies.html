Analysis of Technologies

Timeline

  • Quantum Computer
    • StreamBerry stars, and they start creating a quantum computer capable of producing vast amounts of content for viewers' entertainment at inhumane speeds.
    • Early quantum computer prototypes were tested, and promising results for real-time content generation started to show
    • The Quantum Computer surpasses a significant milestone by successfully simulating complex scenarios that could exponentially speed up content creation.
    • The official launch of the quantum computer-powered AI system, which starts broadcasting personal lives publicly, leading to the show Joan, is awful.
    • Streamberry faces backlash and legal challenges arise as the technology's invasive use is exposed, raising ethical and privacy concerns.
  • Television
    • StreamBerry - compiles data on what you like and makes recommendations
  • Phones
    • Social media apps - algorithms
    • Other listening devices
  • Streaming Services
    • When people signed up for the service, they gave their information away, which is why there are more versions of “Person” is Awful.
    • Everyone in Joan’s life could watch the show, and quickly. Causing Joan’s life to rapidly spiral out of control
